Music Album: Kagayaki - Takagi Masakatsu

I wanted to talk about this record. I’ve found it today and gave it a listen, and it was a well-constructed journey as a Japanese folk album. I loved the instruments and how they combined them together and like i said, the journey was adorable. sometimes in the record you hear people talking and it feels like you’re not alone when listening to the record. I’m having a hard time these days on mental issues and problems like that and this record helped me to ease these times. So, i encourage you to give it a try and talk about your own take on the record and let me know how did you feel about it.
